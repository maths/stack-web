## Optional Additional Packages
Some participants may wish to make the most of their time in Kenya or prefer an all-inclusive arrangement. To support this, we offer a set of optional packages that provide full logistical assistance and access to typical Kenyan experiences.

#### Accommodation and Logistics Package
This package includes pick-up and drop-off at the airport, accommodation at the conference hotel from Sunday 26th July up to Saturday 1st August 2026, hotel dinners, and transport to and from the venue.  
- Cost: **USD 500**  
- Expected stay: **6 nights**  
  - Standard expectation: **Sunday arrival → Saturday departure**  
  - Additional nights may be arranged at **USD 100 per night**
- Deadline for application: **Sunday 26th April 2026**. Late applications may be accepted at a higher rate.

#### Post-Conference Safari
This package includes an all-inclusive weekend safari trip, departing from the conference hotel on Saturday 1st August 2026 and returning to Nairobi in time for evening flights on Monday 3rd August 2026. Two options are available: 

##### Lake Nakuru National Park & Hell’s Gate National Park (Naivasha)
- Cost: **USD 600**  

##### Maasai Mara Safari
- Cost: **USD 1000**

Both options:

- All-inclusive
- Run from **Saturday morning to Monday evening**  
- Include accommodation, transport, meals, park fees, and guided activities
- Deadline for application: **Sunday 15th February**

Full details of each option can be found in the **[Cultural Activities](cultural-activities.md)** page.


#### Child Care and Additional Family Members

Some participants may wish to bring family members with them. The conference team can assist with arranging accommodation, coordinating cultural activities within Nairobi. Arrangements will vary depending on individual needs.

Please contact [STACK2026@idems.international](mailto:STACK2026@idems.international) to discuss arrangements.

## Support Available

The conference aims to be as inclusive as possible and to support participants who do not have institutional funding. Funding is limited and cannot be guaranteed. Priority will be given to participants who contribute to the conference (for example, through the submission of high-quality abstracts or by supporting organisational activities). For Kenyan participants, membership of the Kenya Mathematics Society will also be taken into consideration.

Applications for support are made within the registration form. Several levels of support are available, ranging from subsidised registration fees to full support for travel, accommodation, and fee waivers.

**Application deadline:**  In order to apply for support, the conference registration must be completed by **Sunday 15th February 2026**. Submission of abstract for early consideration must be completed by **Sunday 1st February 2026**.
