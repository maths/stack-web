## Linear Algebra and Custom Libraries in STACK

**Speaker:  Luke Longworth**

**Date:**  Wednesday 29 Apr 2026,   
**Time:**  09.30 am BST (10.30 am CEST), the seminar will last for 1.5 hour   
**Location:**  Online   

In 2025 several libraries of custom maxima functions for linear algebra were included with the release of STACK 4.9.0.  In this talk we will see a selection of these functions and learn how to use them via the stack_include functionality, including some examples from second-year linear algebra at the University of Canterbury, New Zealand. 

We will also briefly discuss the process of contributing to STACK in this way. 
This talk is targeted at STACK users who are already confident in writing questions and would like to expand the pool of useful functions at their disposal.
